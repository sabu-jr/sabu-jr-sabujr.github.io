Cherry Chase Game

"Cherry Chase" is a Unity-based game that offers an engaging and nostalgic experience with 8-bit game aesthetics. The game centers around a fox character whose objective is to collect as many cherries as possible while avoiding hazards such as birds. Players guide the fox as it jumps from one cherry to another, aiming to maximize their cherry collection before the fox falls down.

Key Features:

1. 8-bit Visuals and Audio: The game boasts classic 8-bit visuals and sound effects, providing a retro gaming experience that appeals to both seasoned gamers and newcomers.
2. Challenging Gameplay:Players must skillfully navigate the fox through the falling cherries, making precise jumps to avoid birds and other obstacles.
3. Dynamic Movement: The fox's agile movement mechanics allow for smooth and responsive gameplay, enabling players to aim for high scores.
4. Progressive Difficulty:As players collect more cherries, the game's difficulty increases, adding an element of challenge and excitement to the gameplay.
4. Score Tracking:The game features score tracking to encourage players to compete for the highest cherry collection.


"Cherry Chase" is a testament to my ability to design and implement a game with retro-inspired aesthetics and engaging gameplay mechanics. By utilizing Unity, I was able to create a challenging yet enjoyable game that combines classic visuals and sounds with modern game design principles. This project highlights my proficiency in developing games that offer an immersive and entertaining experience for players.
